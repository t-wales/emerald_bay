About Us

Emerald Bay is a brand new residential gated community in the Ibeju-Lekki area of Lagos, near the $14 billion Lagos Free Trade Zone.

Facilities to be offered include:

  • Perimeter Fence
  • Street Lights
  • Park
  • Road Network
  • Drainage System

Benefits

The benefits of buying at Emerald Bay include but are not limited to these facts:
Emeral Bay is within the vicinity of the famouse Lagos Free Trade Zone, into which the Dangote Group and several other companies have invested over $20 billion so far, the proposed Lekki Airport, the Lekki Deep Sea Port, Greensprings School, Corona School and Pan African University. Not far from Emeral Bay, is the exclusive and uber luxurious Amen Estate, developed by Redbrick Homes International Limited, who are also the developers of the Emeral Bay project. Their enviable track record of excellence in real estate development is a great boon to Emeral Bay.
